Hamburger

Hamburger

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Patty
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
Instructions
  1. Heat a large skillet over medium/high heat.
  2. When the pan is hot, carefully place the patties in the skillet (no need to grease skillet) leaving some space between each patty.
  3. Cook the burgers until nicely seared and they have browned halfway up the sides.
  4. Flip the burgers and cook to your liking.
